Mahadev Janata Secondary School, Sarlahi (महादेव जनता माध्यमिक विद्यालय, सर्लाही) was established in 1976 (2016 BS) as a public academic institute located in Hariwan Municipality – 8, Hariwan, Sarlahi district, Province No. 2 of Nepal. It is affiliated to National Examinations Board (NEB) and approved by Ministry of Education, Science, and Technology (MoEST). This secondary school offers Ten Plus Two (10+2) programs under the Education and Management stream. Simailarly It provides technical education in Plant Science from (Grade 9-12).

Mahadev Janata Secondary School, Hariwan, Sarlahi provides ECD to secondary level education with affordable fees structures. It also offers scholarships schemes for deserving, hardworking and the underprivileged students of the society.

Mahadev Janata Secondary School Spread over three bigaha of land with 7 modern building and 40 rooms. It offers Ten plus two in the Management and Education program since 2015 AD. It has well-equipped science labs for Physics, Chemistry and Biology, and computer. All student has been personal attention and classroom activities are based on student’s participation. It has adopted the English as a medium of instruction from Nursery to grade twelve and Nepali medium from 6 to 12. There are more than one thousand five hundred students studying from ECD to grade 12, of which 52% are girls.

Students are selected from a wide range of socio-economic, cultural and ethnic backgrounds through a competitive Entrance Test. About one-third of the total students are on scholarship sponsored by the Ministry of Education, Nepal. The scholarship is provided to the meritorious and the needy ones from the weak social, economic and geographical backgrounds and from all the seventy-seven districts of Nepal. The main entry of the students takes place in grade 1 and these students go on to take the Secondary Education Examination (SEE) at the end of grade 10. After that, students from the school, together with the selective students from other schools, are selected for the National Education Board (NEB), Nepal.

Initially the school was set up as to educate village boys only. In 2032, the school location was changed and fixed a few buildings. At present there are seven building for boys and girls. It has plan to run students’ residence Hostel by the end of 2082 B.S. The school has been demonstrating that it is not only able to achieve excellent results in the SEE and NEB Examinations, but also achieve results significantly in Games and Sports and Extra Curricular Activities. The school has, therefore, been awarded the prestigious Presidential Running Shield several times by the Hariwan Municipality (a municipal honor for academic excellence) and in effect a public recognition that Mahadev Janata is one of the best schools in this district. In fact, it has been regularly topped the list of good schools in Sarlahi district.

Mahadev Janata Secondary School (MJSS) provides Classrooms, Computer Labs, Multimedia, Journal, Science Labs, Extra-curricular Activities (ECA), Sports, Counselling, Conference, Excursion Educational tours, and scholarships facilities.

In order to cater to the growing need of providing academic counseling services to students, the school has set up a team to provide quality education and empower the students.
